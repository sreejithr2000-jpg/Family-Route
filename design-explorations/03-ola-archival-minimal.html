The Index

Four entries
i.

The living tree കുടുംബവൃക്ഷം

Name a person and point to their parents and partner. The whole family arranges itself — and re-centres on whoever opens it.

ii.

The relation, named ബന്ധം

Choose any two people. The ledger reads back the bond in plain words — paternal or maternal, elder or younger, by blood or by marriage.

iii.

The invitation ക്ഷണം

For a wedding or a function, gather the right names by closeness and number — composed, counted, and ready to send.

iv.

The almanac പിറന്നാൾ

Birthdays and anniversaries as they approach, each with its years — kept so that no one is forgotten.

“Know your people, and you will never be a stranger at your own family’s door.”
